# Job Search Sources

Curated, hyperlinked job-search sources organised by category. Pair each search-query string produced in `10_Target_Roles.md` with one or more of these platforms. Loaded by SKILL.md when generating the target-roles section so links remain accurate and current.

---

## General — Global

- [LinkedIn Jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/) — primary platform; supports keyword, title, and connections-based search
- [Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/) — largest aggregator; strong in North America
- [Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.com/Job/index.htm) — listings with salary and review context
- [ZipRecruiter](https://www.ziprecruiter.com/) — aggregator with employer-side push
- [Monster](https://www.monster.com/) — long-running general board
- [SimplyHired](https://www.simplyhired.com/) — aggregator with salary tools

## Cybersecurity-focused

- [CyberSecJobs](https://www.cybersecjobs.com/) — cyber-only board, US-leaning
- [CyberSeek](https://www.cyberseek.org/) — market-data + heatmap of cyber demand by region/role
- [InfoSec Jobs](https://infosec-jobs.com/) — global cyber-only listings
- [ClearanceJobs](https://www.clearancejobs.com/) — cleared / national-security roles (US)
- [Dice](https://www.dice.com/) — tech and cyber, North America
- [(ISC)² Cyber Job Central](https://isc2.cyberjobcentral.com/) — official board for CISSP / CCSP holders

## Tech & Startups

- [Wellfound (formerly AngelList)](https://wellfound.com/jobs) — startup-heavy listings with founder-direct outreach
- [Hacker News — Who's Hiring](https://news.ycombinator.com/jobs) — monthly thread of YC and tech-aligned hires
- [Built In](https://builtin.com/jobs) — city-scoped tech listings (US)
- [Welcome to the Jungle (Otta)](https://www.welcometothejungle.com/en) — curated tech, strong in EU + remote
- [Hired](https://hired.com/) — tech matching; employers reach out to candidates

## Remote-first

- [We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com/) — 100% remote roles, well-curated
- [Remote.co](https://remote.co/remote-jobs/) — remote roles across categories
- [RemoteOK](https://remoteok.com/) — developer + ops + security remote
- [JustRemote](https://justremote.co/) — filterable remote-only board
- [NoDesk](https://nodesk.co/remote-jobs/) — remote-work resource hub + jobs

## Canada-focused

- [Job Bank Canada](https://www.jobbank.gc.ca/) — federal job-search portal; strong for LMIA roles
- [Canadian Cybersecurity Network](https://canadiancybersecuritynetwork.com/) — community + jobs board for Canadian cyber pros
- [Eluta](https://www.eluta.ca/) — aggregates from Canadian employer career sites
- [Workopolis](https://www.workopolis.com/) — Canadian general board
- [Indeed Canada](https://ca.indeed.com/) — Indeed's Canadian instance
- [CyberAlberta](https://www.cyberalberta.ca/) — provincial industry community (Calgary / Edmonton)

## Ontario-focused

- [WorkInTech](https://workintech.ca/) — Toronto Region Board of Trade tech-jobs portal; strong cyber filter
- [TechTO](https://techto.org/) — Toronto tech community + jobs board + monthly events
- [Communitech](https://www.communitech.ca/) — Waterloo Region tech hub; aggregated postings from member companies
- [Invest Ottawa — Jobs in Ottawa](https://www.investottawa.ca/jobs-in-ottawa/) — Ottawa tech-and-cyber ecosystem job board
- [Ontario Public Service Careers (GoJobs)](https://www.gojobs.gov.on.ca/) — Government of Ontario postings, including Cyber Security Division
- [Cybersecure Catalyst](https://www.cybersecurecatalyst.ca/) — Toronto Metropolitan University cyber accelerator (Brampton); training, hiring, and partner-employer pipeline
- [MaRS Discovery District — Jobs](https://www.marsdd.com/jobs/) — Toronto innovation-hub portfolio companies; many cyber/AI-security roles
- [Toronto Finance International — Careers](https://www.tfi.ca/careers/) — Toronto fintech and bank-cyber roles (Big Five banks, insurers)

## Government & Cleared

- [GC Jobs (Canada)](https://www.canada.ca/en/services/jobs/opportunities.html) — federal public-service postings
- [Canadian Centre for Cyber Security — Careers](https://www.cyber.gc.ca/en/careers) — CSE / CCCS roles
- [USAJOBS](https://www.usajobs.gov/) — US federal jobs portal
- [Intelligence Careers (US IC)](https://intelligencecareers.gov/) — CIA, NSA, ODNI, DIA, etc.
- [UN Careers](https://careers.un.org/) — international civil service roles

## Industry-specific (Energy / OT / Critical Infrastructure)

- [Rigzone](https://www.rigzone.com/jobs/) — oil & gas job board, includes IT / cyber roles
- [Oil and Gas Job Search](https://www.oilandgasjobsearch.com/) — global O&G postings
- [Energy Jobline](https://www.energyjobline.com/) — energy + utilities, global
- [ISA — Automation Career Resources](https://www.isa.org/career-development/career-resources) — ICS/OT roles via ISA's network

## Recruiters & Communities

- [LinkedIn Groups](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/) — join cyber / industry groups for posted roles + intros
- [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/) — local user groups; many post hiring leads
- [BSides Calgary](https://www.bsidescalgary.org/) — community conference; networking and sponsor pipelines
- [InfoSec Exchange (Mastodon)](https://infosec.exchange/) — cyber-pro federated network with `#fediverseHiring`
- [Reddit — r/cybersecurity](https://www.reddit.com/r/cybersecurity/) — monthly hiring threads + community advice

## How to use these in the target-roles file

For each of the 10 roles produced in `10_Target_Roles.md`, the search query goes into one or more of these platforms based on the role's character:

- **Senior, named-employer roles in a specific city** → LinkedIn Jobs, the Canada or Ontario-focused boards.
- **Cleared / government** → ClearanceJobs, USAJOBS, GC Jobs.
- **Cyber-specific roles regardless of region** → CyberSeek (for market intel), InfoSec Jobs, (ISC)² Cyber Job Central.
- **OT / ICS / critical infrastructure** → ISA Career Resources + the energy-specific boards.
- **Remote-tolerant roles** → We Work Remotely, RemoteOK, Welcome to the Jungle.
- **Startups / smaller employers** → Wellfound, HN Who's Hiring, Built In.

Cross-post the same query to 2–3 platforms; aggregator coverage isn't 100%.
